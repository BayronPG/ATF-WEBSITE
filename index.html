About ATF

ATF (A Thousand Flavors) is a company that offers salty food to customers who need to recharge energy after a long and hard day. ATF arose from a conversation in which the group members were thinking about a business idea, and it's funny, especially because the principal business idea was a joke, but we took the basis of that idea and then we formed ATF. The group did financial research and found that after investing 200,000 pesos, a profit of at least double the investment could be obtained if most products were sold as expected. Jessica, who is the manager, always works with the Financial Manager, Product Manager, and Chef (Danna and Leonardo), with the purpose of staying attentive to the evolutionary process of the company, having a safe plan for every setback that may occur, and keeping the product fresh and complete.

Company Name: ATF (A Thousand Flavors)

Team: Jessica Arias, Danna Gonzalez, Leonardo Cuellar

Mission: To provide superior service as a different experience to the palates of our customers at any time.

Vision: To be the most recognized food company in Fontibón by 2030.
